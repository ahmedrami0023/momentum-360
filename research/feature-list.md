# Momentum 360 - Feature Wishlist & Roadmap

> **Core Concept**: A holistic fitness platform that tracks Movement, Nutrition, and Recovery in a unified "Momentum Score". The goal is to reward consistency across all three pillars.

---

## üèóÔ∏è Phase 1: The Foundation (MVP)
*Focus: The "Must Haves" for a functional fitness companion.*

### 1. The Dashboard (The "360 View")
- **Daily Momentum Score**: A unified metric (0-100) combining workouts, calorie adherence, and sleep/steps.
- **Streak Tracker**: Visual chains for daily activity.
- **Quick Add Button**: Floating action button to log Workout, Meal, or Weight in one tap.

### 2. Training (Movement)
- **Workout Logger**:
    - Build Custom Routines.
    - Log Sets, Reps, Weight, RPE.
    - Rest Timer.
- **Exercise Library**: Basic database of exercises with categories (Push, Pull, Legs, Cardio).
- **History**: View past workouts and personal records (PRs).

### 3. Nutrition (Fuel)
- **Macro Tracker**: Log Calories, Protein, Carbs, Fats.
- **Meal Library**: Save favorite meals for quick adding.
- **Water Tracker**: simple tap-to-add interface.
- *Note: We will start without a barcode scanner (expensive API) and focus on manual/AI-text entry.*

### 4. Progress (Data)
- **Body Metrics**: Track weight, body fat %, and measurements.
- **Charts**: Visual graphs for Strength progress and Weight trends.

### 5. Settings
- **Profile**: Age, Height, Weight, Goals.
- **Theme**: Dark/Light mode (Premium aesthetic).

## üöÄ Phase 2: Growth Features (V2)
*Focus: Retention, Social, and Advanced Tech.*

- **AI Personal Trainer**: Generative AI suggestions for workouts based on equipment and time.
- **Social Feed**: Share workouts, friend leaderboards, "Momentum" challenges.
- **Wearable Integration**: Sync with Apple Health / Google Fit.
- **Form Analysis**: Use camera to analyze squat/deadlift form (using simple ML models).
- **Gamification**: Badges, Levels, and Rewards for maintaining streaks.
